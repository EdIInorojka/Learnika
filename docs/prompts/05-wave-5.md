# Codex prompt — Wave 5

 

Implement the paid beta without changing the approved educational core.

 

Required outcomes:

 

- product, plan, subscription and entitlement model;

- payment-provider adapter with signed idempotent webhooks;

- free trial and transparent cancellation;

- upload, AI, OCR, voice and mentor usage limits;

- paywall based on demonstrated value, not fear;

- mentor escalation queue and resolution tools;

- support administration with least privilege;

- content review and publication basics;

- cost dashboards by homework session and cohort, including speech cost;

- backup, restore, retention and incident runbooks;

- production readiness checklist.

 

Do not implement a tutor marketplace, new subject or full school product.

 

Acceptance criteria:

 

- payment and entitlement integration tests pass;

- cancelled subscription preserves records according to policy;

- usage limits are visible;

- mentor recommendations have educational reasons;

- restore and media-deletion tests succeed;

- security, QA and independent review approve.
