# School product — gated future scope

 

## Purpose

Reduce teacher preparation and checking time while turning assessment results into actionable class and individual learning recommendations.

 

## Users

- school administrator;

- teacher;

- class curator;

- learner;

- parent where configured;

- internal support and methodologist.

 

## Organization model

- organization;

- school;

- academic year;

- class;

- subject group;

- teacher assignment;

- student enrollment;

- school license and entitlements.

 

## Teacher capabilities

 

### Assignment builder

- choose curriculum, textbook, sections and skills;

- define duration, difficulty and number of variants;

- use reviewed bank items;

- add original teacher items;

- preview expected answers and rubrics;

- save reusable templates.

 

### Online delivery

- open and close time;

- attempt limits;

- randomization policy;

- accessibility accommodations;

- automatic saving;

- monitoring and incident records.

 

### Print workflow

- generate equivalent PDF variants;

- generate answer sheets, keys and rubrics;

- encode assessment, variant and learner identifiers safely;

- print in low-bandwidth school contexts;

- upload scanned or photographed answer sheets;

- run OMR and handwriting recognition where supported;

- route ambiguity to manual review.

 

### Checking and grading

- deterministic checking for structured answers;

- rubric-assisted checking for open solutions;

- teacher confirmation before final grade in ambiguous cases;

- correction history and audit.

 

### Optional teacher voice comments — later

- short teacher comments may be transcribed and attached to reviewed work;

- teacher confirms the transcript before publication;

- raw audio follows separate retention rules;

- this function is outside the first school beta.

 

### Analytics

- class skill map;

- item difficulty and discrimination indicators;

- frequent error patterns;

- learners needing remediation;

- suggested group and individual practice;

- export consistent with policy.

 

## Data boundary

School tenancy is separate from family tenancy. A school relationship does not automatically grant access to family subscription data or private homework history. Shared data requires a documented policy, legal basis and explicit authorization path.

 

## Pilot success criteria

- teacher preparation time reduced;

- checking time reduced;

- generated variants judged equivalent by method reviewers;

- class analytics understood and used;

- low false-recognition rate with manageable manual review;

- school renewal or paid continuation;

- no critical tenant-isolation defects.

 

## Non-goals of first school beta

- full electronic diary replacement;

- attendance and timetable administration;

- payroll or HR;

- broad government integrations;

- automatic final grading of arbitrary essays or proofs without teacher confirmation.
