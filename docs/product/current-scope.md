# Current production scope

 

## Product stage

Initial product and commercial MVP.

 

## Audience

- learners in grades 7-9;

- parents or legal representatives;

- mathematics mentors and teachers;

- internal administrators and methodologists.

 

## Geography and language

- Russia;

- Russian language;

- initial compliance and infrastructure designed for Russian users and organizations.

 

## Subject

Mathematics:

 

- algebra;

- geometry;

- selected prerequisite skills from earlier grades;

- selected OGE problem types after the core loop is stable.

 

## User goals

- close knowledge gaps;

- understand the current school topic;

- complete homework independently;

- prepare for a test;

- start structured OGE preparation.

 

## Included capabilities

### Family and identity

- parent account;

- child profile;

- parent-child link;

- required consent records;

- grade and school-program settings;

- role-based access.

 

### Curriculum

- seed skill graph;

- textbook catalog and mappings for priority editions;

- school pace setting;

- original explanations and exercises.

 

### Learning

- diagnostic;

- mastery state;

- weekly plan blending three tracks;

- spaced review;

- attempts and steps;

- transfer problems.

 

### Homework helper

- image, screenshot, PDF and manual input;

- metadata stripping and validation;

- task segmentation and normalization;

- supported-problem whitelist;

- deterministic validation for selected algebra types;

- step-by-step hint policy;

- no-answer guard;

- escalation for uncertainty.

 

### Voice input

- optional short Russian recordings up to 60 seconds;

- explicit start, stop and cancel;

- speech-to-text through a provider adapter;

- mathematical phrase normalization;

- editable transcript preview;

- learner confirmation before submission;

- typed-input fallback;

- temporary raw-audio retention and automatic deletion.

 

### Reporting

- weekly parent report;

- problem topics and progress;

- independence and hint use;

- recommended next action.

 

### Human support

- escalation case;

- mentor or teacher comment;

- optional consultation workflow.

 

### Motivation

- internal levels, achievements and progress;

- rewards tied to mastery, error correction, transfer and plan completion.

 

## Explicit non-goals for the first MVP

- all subjects;

- ages below grade 7 or above grade 9;

- EGE;

- a complete standalone game;

- open social network or public global rankings;

- marketplace of tutors;

- full automatic checking of arbitrary handwriting or geometry proofs;

- complete copying of textbooks;

- a full school administration system;

- public procurement integrations;

- multi-country localization;

- use of external game currency;

- realtime voice conversation;

- background or continuous microphone listening;

- text-to-speech tutoring;

- voice biometrics, emotion recognition or speaker profiling;

- long-term raw-audio archive.

 

## Scope change rule

Any addition requires:

 

- named owner;

- problem evidence;

- acceptance criteria;

- cost and risk estimate;

- impact on current wave;

- product-gate approval.
