# Wave 6 open decisions

## Status

Every decision remains `UNRESOLVED_DEFERRED`. The Slice 1 and Slice 2 proposals
are not policy approvals, and none of these rows authorizes identity allocation,
canonicalization, digest generation, review, prerequisite satisfaction or
activation.

## W6-S1-OD-NAMESPACE-OWNER

Name the accountable organizational namespace owner, allocator role,
delegation boundary, revocation process and audit responsibility without
placing personal identity in curriculum artifacts.

## W6-S1-OD-GRAMMAR-APPROVAL

Review the namespace, strand vocabulary, opaque-token alphabet and length,
maximum reference length, case normalization and anchored grammar before any
separate policy approval.

## W6-S1-OD-VERSION-REVISION

Approve the exact change taxonomy only after the future canonical field
inventory is decided. Define which changes create a new identity root, major
version or revision and how stale review state is invalidated.

## W6-S1-OD-COLLISION-CONTROL

Choose the future atomic allocation, uniqueness, reconciliation and incident
response mechanism. No persistence technology or reservation system is
authorized in Slice 1.

## W6-S1-OD-NON-REUSE

Approve permanent non-reuse for both identity roots and full candidate
references, including the treatment of withdrawn, superseded and retired
references. Define reconciliation evidence without creating reservations in
this slice.

## W6-S1-OD-WITHDRAWAL-SUPERSESSION

Approve reference formats, authority, ordering, containment and historical
traceability together with the deferred rollback and withdrawal policy.

## W6-S1-OD-CANONICALIZATION-DIGEST

Approve the field inventory, serialization, algorithm, encoding, domain
separation and reproducibility policy before candidate identity versions can
be bound to immutable content evidence.

## W6-S1-OD-BLUEPRINT-DIGEST-LINKAGE

Approve exact one-slot blueprint pinning, prohibition on deriving candidate
references from slot or registry-entry IDs, and the future candidate
version/revision pins required by the digest registry.

## W6-S1-OD-DATA-EXCLUSION

Approve the identifier data-exclusion policy, opaque allocator-generated key
requirement and validation rules that reject personal, principal, source,
workflow and storage-derived material.

## W6-S1-OD-POLICY-GATE

Define the later policy-review evidence and explicit approval decision. Even
an approved policy must not automatically satisfy the activation prerequisite
or activate the review workflow.

## W6-S2-OD-CANONICAL-FIELD-INVENTORY

schema-version behavior and unknown-field rejection before approval.

## W6-S2-OD-INCLUSION-EXCLUSION

## W6-S2-OD-BYTE-SERIALIZATION

## W6-S2-OD-UNICODE-RUSSIAN-LOCALE

## W6-S2-OD-MATH-NOTATION
